* Mac-Auth Documentation – Guides on MAC address–based authentication, explaining how devices are identified and granted network access.

* GPON – Stands for Gigabit Passive Optical Network, a high-speed fiber-optic broadband technology for delivering internet and voice services.

* ENT – Usually refers to Enterprise Networks, focusing on network infrastructure, management, and services for businesses.

* LTE – Long-Term Evolution, a standard for high-speed wireless communication for mobile devices and data terminals.

* GuestNet – A network service that provides temporary or restricted internet access to visitors without giving full access to the internal network.
