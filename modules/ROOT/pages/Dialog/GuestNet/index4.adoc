== Getting Started

To begin:

. Configure RADIUS and captive portal settings.
. Define guest access policies and profiles.
. Set bandwidth limits and session durations.
. Configure authentication methods (OTP, voucher, etc.).
. Enable logging and monitoring mechanisms.
